  First Aid

  You should always keep some things in your first aid kit, like:


    - Bandaids

    - Gloves

    - Cotton

    - Bandage

    - Antiseptic Cream

    - Medical Tape

    - Tweezers and scissors

    - Medicines for cough, cold and common diseases, etc
