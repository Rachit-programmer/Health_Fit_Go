BMI Classification

If your BMI is:

  • Below 18.5
    – you're in the underweight range.
  • Between 18.5 and 24.9
    – you're in the healthy weight range.
  • Between 25 and 29.9
    – you're in the overweight range.
  • Between 30 and 39.9
    – you're in the obese range.
  • Above 40
    – you're extremely obese.
