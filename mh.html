Test Your Mental Health with A Quiz:

  1. Do you feel overwhelmed by your work?
  2. Do you feel the happy to interact with others?
  3. Do you feel that your work is recognized and appreciated?
  4. Do you feel energized at work?
  5. Do you feel a sense of belonging at work?

Outcome:

    If your answer is 5 Yes - You are enthusiastic

    If your answer is 4 Yes - You are delighted

    If your answer is 3 Yes - You are very happy

    If your answer is 2 Yes - You are happy

    If your answer is 1 Yes - You are somehow happy
